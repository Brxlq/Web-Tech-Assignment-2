About Nikola Tesla

Nikola Tesla (1856–1943) was a Serbian-American inventor, known for his contributions to electricity, AC power, and wireless communication.

Key Events

  1. 1856 – Born in Smiljan
  2. 1884 – Moved to the USA
  3. 1891 – Invented Tesla Coil
  4. 1943 – Passed away in New York

Contributions

  • Alternating Current (AC)
  • Tesla Coil
  • Wireless Transmission
